Caring for your colony

Keep food, water, happiness, and cleanliness balanced. Healthy ants dig new chambers, raise larvae, and grow the colony.

  • The colony day uses real time. The Day counter advances once every 24 hours. Ants still work and rest through their automatic habitat cycle.
  • The lamp is optional. It gives a small warmth boost at night, but leaving it on too long makes the habitat dry.
  • Your progress saves automatically. The colony also changes gently while you are away.
  • Watch the glass. Food carrying, eggs, larvae, tunnel growth, sleeping, and winged ants all appear inside the habitat.

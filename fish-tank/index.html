Caring for Bubbles

Keep hunger, water quality, happiness, and cleanliness balanced. Bubbles will show the most urgent need in a speech bubble and the matching care button will glow.

  • Feed a little at a time. Bubbles swims to each falling flake and eats it.
  • The food jar is interactive. Choose Feed, move the jar over the bowl, then click to shake in three small servings.
  • Day and night are automatic. At night, Bubbles settles near the gravel to sleep.
  • Dirt appears gradually. When you can see specks in the bowl, choose Clean and move your cursor-net through them.
  • The lamp is optional. It wakes Bubbles at night, but leaving it on slowly lowers energy.
  • The bowl grows through decoration. Healthy care unlocks coral, treasure, and a tiny castle.
  • Progress saves automatically. Needs change gently while you are away.
